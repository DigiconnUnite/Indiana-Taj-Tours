# Indiana Taj Tour - Professional Tour Booking Website

## About the Project

Indiana Taj Tour is a comprehensive company website for Indiana Taj Tour, specializing in professional tour bookings across India. This website serves as the official online platform for the company, offering customers detailed information about various tour packages, including Taj Mahal tours, Golden Triangle packages, and day trips. It provides an easy-to-use interface for exploring tour options, making bookings, and contacting the company for personalized travel arrangements.

The project focuses on delivering a user-friendly experience for travelers interested in exploring India's rich heritage and cultural landmarks. With a wide range of tour packages catering to different preferences and budgets, the website helps customers plan their perfect Indian adventure.

## Features

- **Tour Packages**: Extensive collection of professionally curated tour packages including Golden Triangle tours (Delhi, Agra, Jaipur), Taj Mahal visits, and specialized city tours
- **Booking System**: Online booking forms for convenient tour reservations with detailed inquiry submission
- **Contact Options**: Multiple contact forms for general inquiries, booking requests, and customer support
- **Tour Information**: Comprehensive tour pages with detailed itineraries, highlights, and essential information
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly interface that works seamlessly on all devices
- **SEO Optimized**: Search engine friendly pages for better online visibility
- **Professional Layout**: Clean, modern design with engaging content and high-quality images
